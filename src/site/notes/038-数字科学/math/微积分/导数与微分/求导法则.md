满足[[线性性质\|线性性质]]和[[038-数字科学/math/乘法性质\|乘法性质]]

1. $$[u(x)\pm v(x)] = u'(x)\pm v'(x)$$
2. $$[u(x)\cdot v(x)]'=u'(x)v(x)+u(x)v'(x) $$
3. $$[\frac{u(x)}{v(x)}]'=\frac{u'(x)\cdot v(x)-u(x)v'(x)}{v^2(x)}$$
